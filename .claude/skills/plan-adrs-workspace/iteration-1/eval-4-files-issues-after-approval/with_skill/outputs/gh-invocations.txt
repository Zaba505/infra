# One `gh issue create` invocation per planned ADR for the
# self-hosted-application-platform capability. TR-07 is excluded
# because it is already a prior shared decision (Cloudflare -> GCP topology).

  --title "story(adr): tenant isolation boundary — self-hosted-application-platform" \
  --body "$(cat <<'EOF'
### Decision

What mechanism enforces tenant isolation across the data, compute, and telemetry layers such that no tenant can observe or access another tenant's state, secrets, traffic, or telemetry under normal or degraded operation? The decision establishes the isolation primitive (e.g. per-tenant project, namespace, logical partition with policy enforcement) on which every other tenant-scoped decision depends.

### Technical requirements addressed

- [TR-01](../tech-requirements.md#tr-01-tenants-must-be-isolated-such-that-no-tenant-can-read-anothers-state) — tenant isolation

### Parent capability

### Authoring

This ADR will be authored via the `define-adr` skill — one invocation per ADR. The skill will identify research tasks, propose options tied back to the TR-NNs above, and stop for the human to make the final selection.

### Related
